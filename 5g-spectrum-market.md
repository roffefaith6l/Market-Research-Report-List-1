<p><p>The 5G spectrum market comprises various frequency bands, notably C-Band and Millimeter Wave (mmWave) bands. C-Band, typically ranging from 3.7 to 4.2 GHz, offers a balance between coverage and capacity, making it ideal for suburban and urban areas. In contrast, mmWave bands, exceeding 24 GHz, provide extremely high data rates and capacity but have limited range and penetration, requiring dense infrastructure. Together, these bands support diverse applications in connectivity, enabling advancements in IoT, smart cities, and enhanced mobile experiences.</p></p>
