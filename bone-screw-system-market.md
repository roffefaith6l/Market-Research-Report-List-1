<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Bone Screw System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Locking Screws</li><li>Cannulated Screws</li><li>Conventional Screws</li><li>Headless Screws</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone screw system market comprises several types designed for orthopedic procedures. Locking screws provide stable fixation by engaging with a plate, enhancing fracture healing. Cannulated screws feature a hollow design for guided insertion over a wire, facilitating minimally invasive procedures. Conventional screws are solid and used in various applications but may lack the stability of locking types. Headless screws, often used in bone fusion, provide unobtrusive support without a protruding head, ensuring smooth surrounding tissue interaction.</p></p>
