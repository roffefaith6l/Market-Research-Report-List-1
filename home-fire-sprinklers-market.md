<p><strong>The Home Fire Sprinklers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet</li><li>Dry</li><li>Preaction</li><li>Deluge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home fire sprinkler market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Wet Systems**: These are the most common, containing water in the pipes ready to discharge when activated.  </p><p>2. **Dry Systems**: These contain pressurized air instead of water, ideal for areas vulnerable to freezing.  </p><p>3. **Preaction Systems**: These combine features of wet and dry systems, activating under both smoke detection and heat to minimize water damage.  </p><p>4. **Deluge Systems**: These have open sprinkler heads and release large amounts of water simultaneously for high-hazard environments.</p></p>
