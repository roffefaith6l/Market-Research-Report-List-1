<p><p>Airborne Wind Energy (AWE) systems utilize various technologies to harness wind energy at high altitudes. Kites, which are tethered and fly at elevated heights, capture wind energy through dynamic lift. Lifting balloons, or aerostats, ascend into the wind stream and utilize buoyancy along with tethered systems for energy generation. Drones, equipped with turbine mechanisms, maneuver in the wind to optimize energy capture. Each type offers unique advantages for efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and deployment in diverse environments.</p></p>
