<p><ul><li>Single Wavelength</li><li>Double Wavelength</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polarimeters market is categorized into single wavelength and double wavelength types. Single wavelength polarimeters utilize a single light source to measure optical rotation, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for routine applications. In contrast, double wavelength polarimeters employ two light sources to enhance accuracy and sensitivity by compensating for variations in temperature or sample properties. This dual approach allows for more precise measurements, making it suitable for complex analysis in laboratories and industries requiring high reliability.</p></p>
