<p><p>The Display Module Adhesives Market is segmented into two main types: UV Curable and Non-UV Curable adhesives. UV Curable adhesives are activated by ultraviolet light, offering fast curing times and strong bonding, making them ideal for high-precision applications in electronics and displays. In contrast, Non-UV Curable adhesives cure through heat or moisture, providing flexibility in various environmental conditions but typically requiring longer curing times. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring durability and performance in display technologies.</p></p>
