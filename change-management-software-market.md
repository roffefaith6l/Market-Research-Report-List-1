<p><p>Change management software is typically classified into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing organizations to access the software via the internet. This model offers scalability, automatic updates, and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and customization. However, it often requires higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance, making it suitable for organizations with stringent security and compliance needs.</p></p>
