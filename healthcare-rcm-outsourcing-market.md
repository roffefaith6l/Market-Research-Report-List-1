<p><ul><li>Pre-intervention</li><li>Intervention</li><li>Post-intervention</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Healthcare Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) outsourcing can be categorized into three market types: Pre-intervention, Intervention, and Post-intervention. </p><p>Pre-intervention focuses on initial patient registration and insurance verification, ensuring accurate data collection before service delivery. Intervention encompasses the billing and coding processes, where claims are submitted and managed for timely payments. Post-intervention involves follow-up activities, such as denial management and accounts receivable reconciliation, ensuring that revenue is fully realized after services have been rendered. Each phase is critical for optimizing financial performance.</p></p>
