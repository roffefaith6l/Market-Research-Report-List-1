<p><p>The troponin market primarily consists of two types: Troponin I (TnI) and Troponin T (TnT). Troponin I is a cardiac-specific protein released during myocardial injury, making it crucial for diagnosing acute myocardial infarction. Troponin T, while also associated with cardiac distress, can be found in skeletal muscle as well, leading to broader applications. Both types are widely utilized in clinical settings for their high sensitivity and specificity in detecting cardiac damage, driving their market growth in healthcare diagnostics.</p></p>
