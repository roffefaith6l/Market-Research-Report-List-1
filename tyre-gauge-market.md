Dial type gauges offer a more precise reading on a calibrated dial, appealing to enthusiasts. Digital gauges provide the highest accuracy and convenience, displaying readings on an electronic screen, often with added features like backlighting and auto-shutoff. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and usage scenarios in tyre maintenance.</p></p>
