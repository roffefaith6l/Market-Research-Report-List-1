<p><p>Salt Bath Ferritic Nitrocarburizing (SBN) is a surface hardening process that enhances the wear resistance and fatigue life of metal parts, particularly in automotive and manufacturing applications. <p><p>The Salt Bath Ferritic Nitrocarburizing Services market offers two main types: Liquid Salt Bath Ferritic Nitrocarburizing and Gas Salt Bath Ferritic Nitrocarburizing. Liquid Salt Bath involves immersing components in a molten salt solution enriched with nitrocarburizing agents, enhancing surface hardness and wear resistance. In contrast, Gas Salt Bath utilizes gaseous nitrogen and carbon sources in a controlled environment, achieving similar enhancements without immersing the parts in liquid. Both methods improve durability and resistance to corrosion, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
