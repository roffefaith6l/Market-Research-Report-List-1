<p><strong>The Superheaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radiant Superheaters</li><li>Convection Superheaters</li><li>Combined Superheaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The superheaters market comprises three main types: radiant superheaters, convection superheaters, and combined superheaters. Radiant superheaters utilize radiant heat from combustion gases to elevate steam temperature, enhancing thermal efficiency. Convection superheaters, on the other hand, use the flow of hot gases to transfer heat to the steam, optimizing heat recovery. Combined superheaters integrate both methods, maximizing heat transfer and efficiency. These variations cater to diverse industrial applications, improving overall performance in power generation and process heating systems.</p></p>
