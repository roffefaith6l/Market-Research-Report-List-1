<p><p>The graphic processor market is categorized into dedicated graphics cards and integrated graphics processors. Dedicated graphics cards are standalone units, offering high performance for gaming and demanding applications due to their advanced architecture and memory. They are favored by gamers and professionals needing superior graphics capabilities. In contrast, integrated graphics processors are built into the CPU, providing basic graphical functions suitable for everyday tasks and lightweight gaming. While integrated solutions save space and cost, dedicated cards deliver enhanced performance for intensive graphical workloads.</p></p>
