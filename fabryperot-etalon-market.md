<p><ul><li>Solid Etalon</li><li>Air Spaced Etalon</li><li>Piezo Tunable Etalon</li><li>Gires–Tournois Etalon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fabry–Pérot etalon market includes several types, each with distinct applications. Solid etalons utilize a solid medium for light interference, offering durability and stability. Air-spaced etalons use air gaps between mirrors, allowing for tunability and broader wavelength applications. Piezo tunable etalons incorporate piezoelectric materials for precise control over optical path length, enhancing tunability. Gires–Tournois etalons employ a different geometry, optimizing performance for specific wavelengths. These variations cater to diverse needs in optics, telecommunications, and laser applications.</p></p>
