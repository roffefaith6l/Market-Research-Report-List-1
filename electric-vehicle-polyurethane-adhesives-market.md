<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Polyurethane Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based Polyurethane Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-based Polyurethane Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Polyurethane Adhesives market is primarily categorized into two types: water-based and solvent-based polyurethane adhesives. Water-based polyurethane adhesives are eco-friendly, offering low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions, making them suitable for environmentally conscious manufacturing. They provide strong bonding, flexibility, and durability. In contrast, solvent-based polyurethane adhesives contain organic solvents, enabling enhanced bonding strength and performance under extreme conditions, but with higher VOC emissions. Both types play crucial roles in assembling components in electric vehicles, ensuring structural integrity and performance.</p></p>
