<p><strong>The Disc Roller Mill Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spring Loaded</li><li>Hydraulic Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Disc Roller Mill market is primarily categorized into two types: Spring Loaded and Hydraulic Pressure mills. Spring Loaded mills utilize spring mechanisms to exert pressure on the grinding plates, offering flexibility in adjusting the pressure for various materials. In contrast, Hydraulic Pressure mills employ hydraulic systems to provide consistent, high pressure, enhancing the grinding efficiency and output. Both types cater to different processing needs, making them suitable for various industries such as agriculture, mining, and materials processing.</p></p>
