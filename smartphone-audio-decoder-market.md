<p><strong>The Smartphone Audio Decoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lossless Compression</li><li>Lossy Compression</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smartphone audio decoder market is categorized into lossless and lossy compression types. Lossless compression preserves the original audio quality, allowing for perfect reproduction without data loss, making it ideal for audiophiles who prioritize sound fidelity. In contrast, lossy compression reduces file size by eliminating some audio data, resulting in a slight decrease in quality. This format is more commonly used for streaming services and casual listening, as it balances sound quality with storage efficiency, catering to a broader audience.</p></p>
