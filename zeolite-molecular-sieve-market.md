<p><p>Zeolite molecular sieves are categorized based on pore size and structure, influencing their applications in various industries. Type 3A has pores of about 3 angstroms, ideal for selective adsorption of small molecules like water. Type 4A, with 4 angstroms, can trap larger molecules, making it useful in drying and gas separation. Type 5A allows molecules up to 5 angstroms, suitable for larger hydrocarbons. Type X is a versatile commercial zeolite, while ZSM-5 is known for its catalytic properties and is widely used in petrochemical processes. Other types include specialized zeolites designed for specific applications.</p></p>
