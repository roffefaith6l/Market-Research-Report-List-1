<p><p>Intermittent urinary catheters are medical devices used to intermittently empty the bladder in individuals who cannot do so naturally due to various medical conditions. These catheters are designed for single use or reusable options, facilitating improved hygiene and reduced risk of urinary tract infections. The increasing prevalence of conditions that necessitate catheterization, such as spinal cord injuries, multiple sclerosis, and enlarged prostate, contributes significantly to market growth.</p><p>The Intermittent Urinary Catheters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.00% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by advancements in catheter technology, including the development of hydrophilic and antimicrobial-coated catheters which enhance patient comfort and reduce complications. Additionally, the rising awareness of catheter-related infections is prompting healthcare providers to adopt safer and more effective products. </p><p>Trends influencing the market include the shift towards home care services, allowing patients to manage their conditions with minimal clinical intervention, and the increasing focus on quality of life, encouraging innovations that ensure ease of use and comfort.
