<p><p>The Fluid Warming System market encompasses various technologies, primarily ultrasound and electric warming methods. Ultrasound warming systems utilize sound waves to efficiently and evenly heat fluids, promoting rapid temperature elevation without risking overheating. In contrast, electric warming systems rely on resistive heating elements to warm fluids, offering precise temperature control and consistent performance. Both types serve critical roles in clinical settings, ensuring safe and effective fluid administration for patients requiring intravenous therapies or surgical support.</p></p>
