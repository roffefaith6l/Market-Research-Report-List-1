<p><p>The medical hand-held surgical instruments market comprises various tool types essential for surgical procedures. Dilators gently widen passages or openings, whereas graspers securely hold tissues during surgery. Scalpels are sharp instruments for precise incisions. Other instruments include scissors, clamps, and probes, collectively enhancing surgical efficiency and safety, catering to diverse medical specialties and improving patient outcomes during procedures.</p></p>
