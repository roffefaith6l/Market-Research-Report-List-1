<p><p>The residential stair lift market can be categorized into straight stairlifts and curved stairlifts. Straight stairlifts are designed for straightforward, linear staircases, featuring a track that runs from the bottom to the top without any turns. In contrast, curved stairlifts are customized to navigate staircases with bends, landings, or irregular shapes, utilizing a tailored track. Both types enhance mobility and accessibility in homes, providing safe and reliable solutions for individuals with mobility challenges, thereby improving overall quality of life.</p></p>
