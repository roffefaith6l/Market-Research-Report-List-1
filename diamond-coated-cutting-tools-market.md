<p><strong>The Diamond Coated Cutting Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CVD Diamond-coated</li><li>PVD Diamond-coated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diamond-coated cutting tools market is predominantly divided into two types: CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) diamond-coated tools and PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) diamond-coated tools. CVD diamond coatings are created through chemical reactions, forming a robust layer that enhances tool performance, especially in high-temperature applications. In contrast, PVD diamond coatings are applied through a physical vapor process, providing excellent wear resistance and hardness. Both types are favored for their ability to significantly extend tool life and improve machining efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
