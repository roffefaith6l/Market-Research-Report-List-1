<p><strong>The Film Developers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Developer</li><li>Negative Developer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Film developers can be categorized into two main market types: positive and negative developers. Positive developers are used for reversing film processes, where the film captures an image that can be viewed as a positive print directly. This method is often used in slide films. In contrast, negative developers process negative film, producing images that are inverted in color and brightness. Negative films require a further printing step to create positive images, commonly utilized in traditional photography and commercial applications.</p></p>
