<p><strong>The Duct Air Quality Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Sensor</li><li>Resistance Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The duct air quality sensors market primarily comprises capacitive sensors and resistance sensors. Capacitive sensors measure changes in capacitance caused by variations in airborne particulate matter, offering high sensitivity and low power consumption. In contrast, resistance sensors detect changes in electrical resistance due to gas or particulate concentration, providing straightforward and cost-effective solutions for air quality monitoring. Both types serve essential roles in HVAC systems, ensuring optimal indoor air quality and enhancing energy efficiency by detecting contaminants in ducted air systems.</p></p>
