<p><p>The biodegradable collagen implant market comprises several types, including collagen shields, collagen sponges, and collagen gels/hydrogels. Collagen shields are thin, flat membranes used for ocular applications and wound healing, providing a protective barrier. Collagen sponges are porous materials that promote tissue regeneration by facilitating cell infiltration and vascularization. Collagen gels or hydrogels are viscous substances that offer a moist environment for wound healing and can be used in various applications, including drug delivery and tissue engineering, enhancing the healing process.</p></p>
