<p><p>The cement enhancer market is categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Air Entraining Agents** improve concrete's resistance to freeze-thaw cycles by creating microscopic air bubbles, enhancing durability. </p><p>2. **Rust Inhibitors** protect rebar and steel within concrete from corrosion, extending the lifespan of structures. </p><p>3. **Early Strength Agents** accelerate the curing process, enabling quicker load-bearing capacity and facilitating faster project completion. </p><p>4. **Others** include a variety of additives that improve specific properties, such as workability, adhesion, or water repellency, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
