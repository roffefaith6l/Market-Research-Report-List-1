<p><p>The kidney stones management market includes various treatment types, notably Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy (ESWL), Ureteroscopy, and Percutaneous Nephrolithotomy (PCNL). ESWL uses shock waves to break stones into smaller fragments, enabling easier passage. Ureteroscopy involves a small scope to remove or break stones directly within the urinary tract. PCNL is a minimally invasive surgical procedure for larger stones, performed through the skin. Each method has distinct applications based on stone size, location, and patient condition, shaping the market dynamics.</p></p>
