<p><p>The bronchodilator market is categorized into short-acting and long-acting bronchodilators. Short-acting bronchodilators provide rapid relief from acute asthma or COPD symptoms, typically lasting 4 to 6 hours, making them ideal for as-needed use. Long-acting bronchodilators offer sustained relief for 12 hours or more, designed for regular use to manage chronic respiratory conditions. Both types are crucial in optimizing patient care, improving lung function, and enhancing the quality of life for individuals with obstructive airway diseases.</p></p>
