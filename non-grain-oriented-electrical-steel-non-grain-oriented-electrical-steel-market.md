<p><p>Non-Grain-oriented Electrical Steel (NGOES) is a specialized material primarily used in electrical applications, particularly in transformers and electric motors. This characteristic makes it ideal for applications requiring efficient electromagnetic performance over a range of orientations.</p><p>Currently, the Non-Grain-oriented Electrical Steel Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient electrical equipment and advancements in renewable energy technologies.
