<p><strong>The Photomask Review Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photomask Review Machine market features two primary types: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB) methods. The DD method compares individual dies on a photomask against each other to detect variations and defects, ensuring quality across multiple designs. In contrast, the DB method involves comparing a die to a pre-existing database of verified designs, allowing for more comprehensive validation against extensive records. Both methods enhance the accuracy and reliability of photomask production in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
