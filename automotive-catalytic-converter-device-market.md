<p><strong>The Automotive Catalytic Converter Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three-way Catalysts (TWC)</li><li>Lean NOx Catalysts (LNC)</li><li>Lean NOx Trap (LNT)</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)</li><li>Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive catalytic converter device market consists of various types designed to reduce harmful emissions from vehicles. Three-way catalysts (TWC) manage CO, unburned hydrocarbons, and NOx in gasoline engines. Lean NOx catalysts (LNC) and Lean NOx traps (LNT) focus on reducing NOx emissions in lean-burn engines. Selective catalytic reduction (SCR) improves NOx conversion efficiency using a reductant like urea. Diesel oxidation catalysts (DOC) facilitate oxidation of carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons in diesel engines, enhancing overall emission control.</p></p>
