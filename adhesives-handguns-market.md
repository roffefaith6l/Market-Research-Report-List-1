<p><p>The adhesives handguns market is primarily categorized into two types: hot melt and cold glue. Hot melt adhesives are thermoplastic materials that are melted and applied in a liquid state, rapidly solidifying upon cooling, making them ideal for quick applications in various industries. Cold glue, on the other hand, is water-based and remains in a pliable state, suitable for applications requiring longer bonding times and adjustments during assembly. Each type serves distinct purposes in manufacturing, packaging, and crafting.</p></p>
