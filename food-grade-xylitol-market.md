<p><p>The food-grade xylitol market is primarily categorized into two types: crystal granule and powder forms. Crystal granule xylitol features a coarse texture, ideal for baking and sweetening beverages, enhancing texture and bulk in recipes. In contrast, powder xylitol is finely milled, making it suitable for applications requiring quick dissolution, such as in beverages or as a sugar substitute in sauces. Both types serve diverse consumer preferences, supporting a growing demand for natural sweeteners in the food industry.</p></p>
