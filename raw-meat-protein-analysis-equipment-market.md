<p><p>The raw meat protein analysis equipment market utilizes various technologies to assess the protein content in meat. DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) technology provides precise measurements of protein by analyzing the absorption of X-rays in meat samples. In contrast, NIR (Near-Infrared Reflectance) technology uses light to estimate protein levels through spectral analysis, offering rapid, non-destructive testing. Both methods are essential for quality control in the meat industry, ensuring accurate nutritional information and compliance with food safety standards.</p></p>
