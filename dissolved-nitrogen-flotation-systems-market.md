<p><p>Dissolved Nitrogen Flotation Systems (DNFS) are advanced wastewater treatment technologies that utilize nitrogen gas to separate suspended solids and contaminants from liquids. The process enhances the efficiency of waste removal by generating fine nitrogen bubbles, which adhere to particles, causing them to rise to the surface for easy removal. These systems are increasingly gaining traction in various industries, including municipal wastewater treatment, food and beverage, and oil and gas.</p><p>The Dissolved Nitrogen Flotation Systems Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period, driven by increasing regulatory pressures for wastewater treatments and a growing focus on sustainable practices.
