<p><p>Thermoset resin composites include Sheet Molding Compound (SMC) and Bulk Molding Compound (BMC), both of which are widely used in various industries. SMC is produced in sheets, allowing for easy molding and high-strength applications, suitable for automotive and construction sectors. BMC, on the other hand, is a premixed paste containing short fibers, ideal for intricate shapes and complex designs. Both types offer excellent thermal stability, chemical resistance, and durability, making them popular choices for applications requiring robust performance.</p></p>
