<p><p>The property and casualty reinsurance market consists of two primary distribution types: intermediary selling and direct selling. Intermediary selling involves brokers who facilitate the placement of reinsurance contracts between insurers and reinsurers, providing expertise and access to multiple markets. In contrast, direct selling occurs when reinsurers engage directly with insurers, streamlining negotiations and decision-making. Both methods aim to optimize risk transfer and pricing, catering to the varying needs of insurers seeking protection against potential losses.</p></p>
