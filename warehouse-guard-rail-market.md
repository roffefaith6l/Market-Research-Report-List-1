<p><p>The warehouse guard rail market is categorized into two main types: double rail and single rail systems. Double rail guard rails consist of two horizontal rails, providing enhanced protection against collisions and ensuring greater safety for personnel and equipment. In contrast, single rail guard rails involve a single horizontal rail, offering a more cost-effective solution, suitable for areas with lower collision risks. Both types serve to enhance workplace safety by creating physical barriers to prevent accidents and protect assets.</p></p>
