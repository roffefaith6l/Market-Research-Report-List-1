<p><p>The hygienic pumps market is primarily categorized into three types: Centrifugal Type, Positive Displacement, and Other types. Centrifugal pumps utilize a rotating impeller to move fluids, making them ideal for low-viscosity applications. Positive displacement pumps, on the other hand, trap a fixed amount of liquid and force it through the outlet, suitable for high-viscosity fluids. Other market types may include diaphragm and peristaltic pumps, catering to specialized applications requiring stringent hygiene standards in food and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
